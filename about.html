About AR Autoworks

About Our Founder

Ashley: A Journey of Passion and Perseverance

Ashley, the visionary behind AR Autoworks, started his journey with a simple dream and an unwavering determination. Growing up in a modest neighborhood, Ashley was fascinated by the mechanics of cars. His passion for automobiles was evident from a young age, as he spent countless hours in his father’s small garage, learning the intricacies of car repair and maintenance.

Despite numerous challenges and limited resources, Ashley's resolve never wavered. He took up various jobs to fund his education and eventually earned a degree in automotive engineering. With a burning desire to make a difference, Ashley founded AR Autoworks with the mission to provide top-notch automotive services to his community.

Today, AR Autoworks stands as a testament to Ashley's hard work, dedication, and the belief that with passion and perseverance, any dream can become a reality. His story inspires many young individuals to follow their dreams, no matter how humble their beginnings.

Our Vision and Mission

At AR Autoworks, our vision is to provide exceptional automotive services with a commitment to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Our mission is to be the go-to destination for all your vehicle needs, offering comprehensive services that ensure safety, performance, and reliability.

Our Story

Founded in 2005, AR Autoworks started as a small garage with a passion for cars and a dedication to customer service. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service automotive workshop, serving the Cape Town community with expertise and care. Our history is built on a foundation of trust, hard work, and a love for automobiles.

Our journey from a modest garage to a renowned service center is a testament to our commitment to quality and our customers. We continually strive to uphold our values of integrity, professionalism, and excellence in every service we provide.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of automotive services, including diagnostic services, routine maintenance, electrical system repairs, engine repairs, brake services, suspension and steering services, detailing, and custom modifications. Our skilled technicians are dedicated to providing top-notch services that keep your vehicle running smoothly and safely.

Using the latest diagnostic tools and techniques, we ensure high-quality repairs and maintenance. Whether it's a simple oil change or a complex engine repair, we treat every job with the utmost care and attention to detail.

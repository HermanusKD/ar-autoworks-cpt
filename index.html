About AR Autoworks

At AR Autoworks, we believe that every drive should be an adventure, not just a routine. From our early days to becoming a cornerstone in the automotive industry, our journey is marked by a deep passion for cars and a relentless commitment to quality. Curious about how we transformed from a small garage into a trusted name? Read More

Our mission is to ensure that you and your passengers have a safe and enjoyable journey. We are committed to providing high-quality, reliable, and comprehensive auto repair services, designed to keep your vehicle in top condition and protect you from potential dangers on the road.

Our focus at AR Autoworks is on delivering convenient, cost-effective, and expertly executed repairs. Whether you need engine rebuilds, suspension servicing, electrical work, or any other type of auto repair, our skilled team is here to help.

Please feel free to contact us if you require further information or assistance. We are dedicated to meeting your automotive needs with excellence.

Our Vision and Mission: At AR Autoworks, our vision is to provide exceptional automotive services with a commitment to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Our mission is to be the go-to destination for all your vehicle needs, offering comprehensive services that ensure safety, performance, and reliability.

Our Story: Founded in 2005, AR Autoworks started as a small garage with a passion for cars and a dedication to customer service. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service automotive workshop, serving the Cape Town community with expertise and care. Our history is built on a foundation of trust, hard work, and a love for automobiles.

Our journey from a modest garage to a renowned service center is a testament to our commitment to quality and our customers. We continually strive to uphold our values of integrity, professionalism, and excellence in every service we provide.

Our Services: We offer a wide range of automotive services, including diagnostic services, routine maintenance, electrical system repairs, engine repairs, brake services, suspension and steering services, detailing, and custom modifications. Our skilled technicians are dedicated to providing top-notch services that keep your vehicle running smoothly and safely.

Using the latest diagnostic tools and techniques, we ensure high-quality repairs and maintenance. Whether it's a simple oil change or a complex engine repair, we treat every job with the utmost care and attention to detail.
